Member Enumeration Documentation

enum xmm::ProbabilisticModel::CALLBACK_FLAG

Flags for the Callback called by the training algorithm.

Enumerator
TRAINING_RUN 

Training is still running

TRAINING_DONE 

Training is done without error

TRAINING_ERROR 

An error occured during training (probably convergence issue)

TRAINING_ABORT 

The training has been cancelled.

TRAINING_ALLDONE 

The training of all classes has finished

void xmm::ProbabilisticModel::train ( )

Main training method based on the EM algorithm.

the method performs a loop over the pure virtual method train_EM_update() until convergence. The train_EM_update method computes both E and M steps of the EM algorithm.
