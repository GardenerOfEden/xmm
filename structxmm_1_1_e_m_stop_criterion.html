unsigned int xmm::EMStopCriterion::maxSteps

Maximum number of iterations of the EM algorithm.

If this value is superior to minSteps, this criterion is used. Otherwise, only the 'percentChg' criterion applies.

double xmm::EMStopCriterion::percentChg

log-likelihood difference threshold to stop the EM algorithm.

When the percent-change in likelihood of the training data given the estimated parameters gets under this threshold, the EM algorithm is stopped.
